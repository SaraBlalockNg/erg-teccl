Fatigue is one of the most common complains, more people complain of fatigue today than in the old days.
The causes of modern-day fatigue are diverse and only rarely relate to excessive physical exertion.
Fatigue can be classified three main categories.
Physical: physical fatigue is a pleasant tiredness.
The result of overworking your muscles is making metabolic waste products accumulate in your blood and sap your strength, the cure is rest.
Pathological: here fatigue is a warning sign or consequence of some underlying physical disorder.
Anyone who feels drained of energy for weeks on end should have a thorough physical checkup.
Psychological: emotional problems and conflicts, especially depression and anxiety, are the most common causes of prolonged fatigue, fatigue may represent a defense mechanism and a body's safety valve.
Machine cannot help deal with fatigue, instead, we may try to develop a proper diet, do regular conditioning exercise, have a suitable amount of sleep and know ourselves well.
As long as there is the existence of fatigue, people would have to go to eliminate it.
Different people will take different approaches, just as the famous works: "One thousand people have one thousand Hamlet".

I'll introduce something about shadow play.Shadow play or shadow puppetry is an ancient form of storytelling and entertainment using opaque, often articulated figures in front of an illuminated backdrop to create the illusion of moving images.Shadow play is a popular folk drama in ancient China, and it is the ancestor of world films.During a shadow play, puppeteers hide themselves behind a white curtain and move stick-mouted puppets, while also narrating the story through folk song.Performances are generally accompanied by musicians playing drums and stringed instruments.At that time, it brought people pleasure as modern movies and TVs do.How this play originate?It originated during the Han Dynasty when one of the concubines of Emperor Wu of Han died from an illness.He was devastated and summoned his court officers to bring his beloved back to life.The officer made a shape of the concubine with donkey leather.The emperor thoroughly enjoyed the performance, after which shadow play become a main feature of the royal court and imperial China.
In modern society, it's very difficult to find the mysterious drama again because of the development of Tvand Movie.Like some other folk arts, it gradually becomes the one that we can only see in the TV.But the government still try their best to protect and develop this old art.The Chinese Ministry of Culture has applied to UNESCO for shadow puppetry to be recognized as an intangible part of world cultural heritage, following UNESCO's decision to remove the limit on the number of applications for cultural heritage protection.
